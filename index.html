About Me

I'm Rajesh, the eye behind the lens and the heart behind R A Snaps. What started as a passion for capturing fleeting moments has grown into a creative journey dedicated to telling visual stories with authenticity, style, and soul.

At R A Snaps, I specialize in turning everyday emotions into timeless images. Whether it’s a candid wedding moment, a powerful portrait, or the quiet magic of nature, I believe that the most beautiful photographs are the ones that speak without words.

With a blend of technical precision and artistic intuition, I work closely with clients to ensure every session is personal, comfortable, and unforgettable. Every shoot is more than just a job — it’s an opportunity to create memories that last a lifetime.
